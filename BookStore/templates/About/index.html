About Us

Are you searching for books to read in Nepal? But don’t have enough money to buy the new books to read. Trying to get a book at a very cheap price is a great hassle for everybody who is looking forward to reading new books. Now you don’t have to worry about this problem. This application is here to solve your problem on getting the books you need at a very cheap price ("Everything You Should Know About Thrift books", 2022). Most of the used books are getting ruin on the shelf and many readers are out there not getting the chance to read books due to the price of new books. So, it provides quality books to our customers. It helps save the secondhand books in Nepal. For people looking to sell their books, this web application accepts those books and make them available in the store for people to buy. Its focus is to share the power of the books and connect books with people by making discovering and buying books easy, entertaining, informative, and socially engaging ("Buy Used Books Online", 2022).
